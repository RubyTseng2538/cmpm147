Experiment 03: Alternate Worlds

Description

In this experiment, I attempted to create a convincing snow map while incorporating variation.

Technical

To enhance the generated appearance of the map while maintaining its visual appeal, I introduced more randomness to the location where trees are generated. However, the lake generation remained mostly consistent in the code. To introduce variation to both tree generation and the lake, I randomly added ground code after the trees and lake were generated.

Reflection

I encountered some challenges in achieving variation in the map's appearance. Attempting to make the lake generation more varied led to issues with auto-tiling, particularly in locations where the provided tiles could not replicate the desired appearance. As a result, I had to impose more restrictions on the lake. However, auto-tiling for trees around the original trees was more straightforward, and it enhanced the forest's density.
